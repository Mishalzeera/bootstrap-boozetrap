GAMBOLING


Or, the Sorrowful Finale Nail in the Casket!!!


In Gamboling, Mr. Greaves acquired as much as he was divestede of. The fever with which he was engripped was so as to incapacitate him for any useful function in his now former life. His beloved and faithful wife of noble birth and fine character, in proud but sorrowful determination, returned to the house of her family. His home was sold in absentia, upon Officiale order to pay the final sums of his debts to His Majesties Revenue.


the Mysteriouse and Awesome death of the Fallen Mr. Greaves

The Deathe of Mr. Greaves


On his Third and Sixtieth anniversary, while at his diabolical Game of gambols, the Harlotes snowy bosom foremoste in his thoughts, there came a terrible and grevious blast of HORNS from the river Thames! Numerous of his cohort atteste with their own blackened Eyes to this Daye the sight of a terrible and Wrothful Angel, who, seizing the Fallen Greaves by his matted forelocke, growled mysterious syllables Three before dragging the unfortunate Soule away.
