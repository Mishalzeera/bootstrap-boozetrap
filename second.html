Allowing the habit and Excesses of DRINK to Claime his best Nature...


Mr. Greaves was of the habit of indulging in small quantities of white wine with his Sunday dinnere. As he was now in the Public House, he began by ordering his usual wine by the glass. The pleasant feeling of intoxication was by no means a strong incentive upon his true and solid foundational character at firste. But, his association with the fallen types who frequented The Booze Trap Public House led to him developing and indulging his weak but nascent proclivity until it Manifestede as a regular indulgence of Stronge Gin with not any water.


Foule and Loutishe Gin drinkers brazenly Drunken and in Disarraye


And then came the Pointe at which Fortune, having ascertained Mr. Greaves association with the Habits and Company of fallen people, assigned him Her most Wickede of tests.
