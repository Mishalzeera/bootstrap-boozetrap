Encountering the Harlote Jeanette De Blaise


Mr.Greaves, upon Castinge his lot with the Corruptor of the innocent, found himself often in the company of loose Women of negotiable Virtues. His open and personable Nature being what it was, he came soon the malicious Attentions of those Harlots who, upon setting their design regarding a man of means, relenquish not their design until it has been fully executed.

Among these detestable Harpies was that Siren of uncontestable outer beauty, matched only by the depravity of her inner nature. The Siren, well known to all, who makes the very Whores of Hell blush in shame, was the Harlote Jeanette De Blaise.



The Mantrappe, the Harlote Jeanette De Blaise!!!

the Harlote De Blaise and her unHoly desire for Wealth and Jeweles


The Harlote De Blaise, seeing that taking the gifts of Besotted and undiscerning Gentleman was easier than working an honest trade, had an Insatiable hunger and luste for Wealth and Jeweles. Ensnaring unwitting Public School boys of the upper class and divesting them of their pockete monies was indeed her daily Faire. For her own wicked Pleasures she enjoyed inflaming such as the former respected Gentleman Mr. Greaves, and trying her creative Endevoure to his Fates, bending and twisting opportune moments to her own Dark provenance - in hopes of a spouting forth of an ill-gotten streame of wealth.


Favouring the Fallen Greaves with a kiss, the sight of a thigh, the scent of her Kercheife dipped as it was in potions to Emboggle and Infiltrate his very grip on sanity, she instilled in him the passionate wish to acquire funds as quickly as possible, which, as so often happens, drove him to Gamboling!
